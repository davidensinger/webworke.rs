Webworke.rs runs on Jekyll, a blog-aware, static site generator, with some help from Grunt. The site was scaffolded by Yeoman and generator-jekyllrb, the latter being a project of @robwierzbowski. Changes have since been made to the scaffolding of the site, but I still check in on the original project to stay up-to-date on the commits.

Grunt Dependencies

By default there are more than two dozen dependencies installed with generator-jekyllrb, but these are the most noteworthy ones, as well as a couple that I’ve a:

  • grunt-jekyll by @dannygarcia.
  • grunt-autoprefixer by @nDmitry based off Autoprefixer by @ai
  • grunt-imageoptim by @JamieMason based off ImageOptim, ImageAlpha, JPEGmini, the two former by @pornel.

Jekyll Plugins

  • jekyll-picture-tag by @robwierzbowski and based off Picturefill by @scottjehl.
  • Category and Tag Paging and Feeds by @realjenius.

Front End Dependencies

Bower, “A package manager for the web.”

  • matchMedia.js by @paulirish
  • Mousetrap by @ccampbell
  • Picturefill by @scottjehl

Styles

Sass most recently by @nex3 and @chriseppstein

  • Idiomatic CSS by @necolas
  • inuit.css by @csswizardry
  • KNACSS-Sass a port of @RaphaelGoetter's KNACSS framework by @HugoGiraudel
  • MVCSS by @nickawalsh and @drewbarontini
Typography: Fonts http://holdfastfoundry.com/shop/industry/, http://vollkorn-typeface.com/
